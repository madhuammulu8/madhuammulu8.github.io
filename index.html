Working on developing a solar panel cleaning robot using firebird V was my final year project that was aimed at increasing the efficiency of solar panels. Through the project, I developed a rotating cylindrical brush made of microfiber to enable dry cleaning as most solar panel fields are located in areas with water scarcity like deserts. Moreover, working alongside experienced professors on enhancing the efficiency of solar panels present in several college blocks was a unique experience that gave me the opportunity to contribute to real-world systems. I published the results of the project as a research paper titled ‘Automatic Solar panel cleaning robot using Firebird V’ in the IJESC Publications.
